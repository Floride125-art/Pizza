About Us

Welcome 2 getPizza

getPizza Restaurant is a start up business that is located in Rwanda, Kigali city. It mostly relies on online delivery services whereby we bake Pizza and deliver them to those who wants for free.

We started last year in 2019 whereby we used to work in the small restaurant without offering delivery services, but after seeing that many people are in need of our Pizza and it hard for some on them to travel to our restaurant we decided to start free Pizza delivery regardless where people is living in Rwanda not actually outside of it.

Our vision and Mission

We take pride in making a perfect pizza and providing courteous and helpful service on time all the time. Every customer says, "I'll be back!

We strive to be the best among the companies that deliver Pizza to the clients in this Easter Africa Region
